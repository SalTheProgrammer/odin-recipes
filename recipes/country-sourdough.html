Country Sourdough Bread Recipe

Description

Here is a simple sourdough bread that will blow your mind. The taste is very pleasant and the texture very succulent. It's best to enjoy this with a slice of butter and jam. You need a strong sourdough starter, good bread flour, salt, filtered water, and patience. That's it!

Ingredients

  • 100grams fed and mature sourdough starter
  • 375grams water
  • 450grams bread flour
  • 50grams whole wheat flour
  • 10grams salt

Steps

  1. Dissolve salt into water
  2. Stir sourdough starter into salt and water mix
  3. Combine flour and mix well by hand
  4. Knead for a few minutes or slap and fold until dough comes together and all dried flour is gone
  5. Move dough into a new container
  6. For every 30 minutes, gently fold the dough onto itself. This needs to be done 4 times in a span of 2 hours
  7. Let the dough ferment at room temperature for the next 2 hours
  8. After 4 hours, dough should pass the window pane test and starts to smell a little sour
  9. Transfer dough onto a baneton that has been lightly dusted with rice flour
  10. Cover and move baneton to fridge and let it sit overnight
  11. The next day, preheat oven to 220C with a dutch oven in it
  12. Take out dough from baneton and score the top of the dough
  13. Carefully transfer the dough to dutch oven and bake covered for 20 minutes
  14. Lower the temperature of oven to 180C and uncover the dutch oven, bake for another 20 minutes
  15. When done, take out bread from dutch oven and let cool for at least one hour
